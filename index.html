Frequently Asked Questions

Our platform supports the following exchanges Binance, Bitfinex, Coinbase Pro, Hitbtc, Kraken, Kucoin, Okex.

There is no per-transaction fee or commission for any trading transaction from our platform. However, Exchanges may charge fees or commissions depending on the type of order executed.

For Taxes we are currently supporting USA and United Kingdom for generating appropriate tax forms. We will be including many more countries in the future.

Payment can be made using cryptocurrency or credit/debit card. We use Coinbase Commerce and Stripe for secure payment processing. All information is submitted securely over SSL.

Since we offer a free 14-day trial, we do not provide refunds once subscribed to a plan. Please note that all plans are auto-renewed (except when paid with cryptocurrency). If you purchase a Trading plan or any of the Tax plans, it's implied that you agree with the terms and conditions and accept that there are no refunds.
